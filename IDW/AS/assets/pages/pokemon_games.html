Pokémon Scarlet e Violet

Pokémon Scarlet and Pokémon Violet are the first open-world RPGs in the Pokémon series. As you enjoy the hallmarks of the series - meeting, teaming up with, battling, training, and trading Pokémon - you'll be able to immerse yourself in a newly evolved adventure

Pokémon Legends: Arceus

Pokémon Legends: Arceus, developed by GAME FREAK for the Nintendo Switch system, presents an adventure in the Hisui region—a land from an age long ago that will one day become known as the Sinnoh region.

Pokémon Brilliant Diamond and Pokémon Shining Pearl

The Sinnoh region is calling in Pokémon Brilliant Diamond and Pokémon Shining Pearl, a pair of role-playing game adventures for Nintendo Switch. Developed by ILCA, Inc. and directed by Junichi Masuda of GAME FREAK and Yuichi Ueda of ILCA, these games are updates of the classic Nintendo DS titles Pokémon Diamond and Pokémon Pearl.

Pokémon UNITE

Pokémon UNITE is a strategic team battle game being developed jointly by The Pokémon Company and Tencent Games’ TiMi Studios. Pokémon UNITE is planned as a cross-platform game for Nintendo Switch and mobile devices and will be free-to-start.
